## Founder Story

**Jesus Vargas** (also known as @eltintero on social media) is the founder and CEO of LowCode Agency. His journey is marked by resilience and innovation:

### Early Life
- Born into an upper-class Mexico City family (father owned successful business, grandfather owned one of Mexico's biggest companies)
- At age 5, pulled into isolation when mother joined a religious doomsday cult
- Homeschooled with evangelical curriculum, forbidden from TV, music, or playing with cousins
- Family lost everything in 1994 Mexican financial crisis
- Survived on rice and beans, fed dog tortillas
- Secretly listened to news radio at night - his only connection to outside world

### Tech Heritage
His grandfather, Jorge Espinosa Mireles, founded Printaform in 1961:
- Started with office stationery, expanded to tech devices
- Developed world's first portable computer (Printaform Columbia)
- Dominated Mexican computer market, beating Apple and IBM
- Partnered with Bill Gates to pre-load Windows in Spanish
- Kidnapped in 1992, held for $15M ransom
- Lost competitive advantage when NAFTA was instituted

### Path to LowCode
- At 13, witnessed failed rapture prediction by his cult
- Got 30 minutes of supervised internet daily, helped translate WordPress to Spanish
- At 18, escaped to Mexico City for college
- Invited to work at grandfather's company (only grandchild invited)
- Doubled company headcount, got products into Walmart, CVS, Office Max
- Left when grandfather wouldn't guarantee succession
- Experimented with various businesses (fiber optic cables, solar panels)
- 2019: Discovered Glide on Product Hunt - "Build an app from a Google Sheet"
- Became THE Glide expert, answering every forum question
- First major project: Built Fortune 500 inventory system in a weekend for $300 (they expected $300k)
- Moved to US and founded LowCode Agency

### Personal Philosophy
"Every day I wake up on a ledge, about to fall down. I think that something is going to happen, and I'm going to lose my business. So I just work. I just focus on growing. There's nothing else."

Now a committed husband and father, driven by the weight of responsibility after seeing his father and grandfather lose everything.

## Development Process

### Standard Timeline
- **Internal Business Apps**: Starting at 4 weeks
- **SaaS/Marketplaces/Native Apps**: 8-12 weeks
- Timelines adjust based on complexity and scope

### Process Steps
1. **Discovery Call** - Understanding client needs and business goals
2. **Business Analysis** - Refinement stage led by business analyst
3. **Scope of Work & Proposal** - Detailed project documentation
4. **Project Kickoff** - Onboarding and team introductions
5. **Low-Fidelity Wireframes** - Basic layout and functionality mapping
6. **High-Fidelity Design** - Complete UI/UX design
7. **Development** - Milestone-based development approach
8. **Quality Assurance** - Testing and bug fixes
9. **Client Review** - Feedback and iterations
10. **Project Handoff** - Training and documentation
11. **Long-term Partnership** - Ongoing support and evolution
