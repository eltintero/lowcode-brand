# About Me — Jesus Vargas

## Who I Am

I'm Jesus Vargas, founder and CEO of **LowCode Agency** — a 40-person software development agency that builds apps 90% faster and cheaper than traditional development. I go by **@eltintero** on social media.

I split my time between **Miami and Cancun**. My team is fully remote — mostly in Latin America, with a few in Europe and 3-4 in Asia.

I'm an entrepreneur first, a builder second, and an educator third. I don't come from a technical background. I discovered no-code tools in 2019 when I found Glide on Product Hunt and built a mini-CRM for my solar panel company in 3 hours. My first paid project was a $1,000 Airbnb management app. Six years later, we've built 350+ apps for clients like Sotheby's, American Express, Coca-Cola, Medtronic, Zapier, and GAF.

## Background

My story is shaped by extremes. I was born into an upper-class Mexico City family — my grandfather, Jorge Espinosa Mireles, founded Printaform in 1961 and developed the world's first portable computer, beating Apple and IBM in the Mexican market. He partnered with Bill Gates to pre-load Windows in Spanish.

At age 5, my mother joined a religious doomsday cult. I was pulled into isolation — homeschooled with evangelical curriculum, no TV, no music, no contact with cousins. Then the 1994 Mexican financial crisis hit and my family lost everything. We survived on rice and beans.

At 13, I watched the cult's rapture prediction fail. At 18, I escaped to Mexico City for college. My grandfather invited me to work at his company — I doubled their headcount and got products into Walmart, CVS, and Office Max. When he wouldn't guarantee succession, I left and started experimenting: fiber optic cables, solar panels, and eventually no-code.

Now I'm a husband and father. The weight of responsibility — having seen my father and grandfather lose everything — drives me every day.

## What LowCode Agency Does

We build custom software for **growing SMBs** and **early-stage founders** using the best tool for each job:

- **Low-code platforms**: Glide, Bubble, FlutterFlow, Webflow, WeWeb
- **High-code stack**: React, Next.js, Vercel, Supabase, Cursor
- **AI & automation**: n8n, custom AI integrations, various AI APIs
- **Design**: Figma for all design work

We serve two primary markets: SMBs needing internal tools (CRMs, dashboards, field apps, AI agents) and founders launching digital products (MVPs, SaaS, marketplaces, AI tools). Projects start at $15K for internal apps and $30K for SaaS/marketplaces/native apps.

Our philosophy: "Fast isn't enough. We build forward, think strategically, and run as a product team."
